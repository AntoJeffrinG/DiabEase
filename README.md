## 📌 Project Overview  

**DiabEase** is an AI-powered health assistant designed to enhance **diabetes management** through intelligent insights and real-time monitoring.  

By leveraging **machine learning and AI-driven analytics**, DiabEase empowers both patients and healthcare providers with **personalized recommendations** and **proactive intervention strategies** for improved diabetes care.  

### 🔹 Key Features  

✅ **Personalized Insulin Dosage Prediction** – Utilizes ML models to recommend **optimal insulin dosages** based on real-time patient data.  

✅ **Real-time Monitoring & Alerts** – Continuously tracks blood glucose levels and sends **instant alerts** to caregivers in case of critical fluctuations.  

✅ **Tailored Dietary Recommendations** – Provides **AI-driven meal suggestions** to help maintain balanced glucose levels.  

✅ **AI-powered Chatbot (MediBot)** – Offers instant responses to **diabetes-related queries**, enhancing patient awareness and self-care.  

✅ **Diabetic Retinopathy Detection** – Integrates a **CNN-based model** to analyze retinal scans for **early detection** of diabetic retinopathy.  

By integrating cutting-edge **AI technology** with **user-friendly healthcare solutions**, **DiabEase** aims to **revolutionize diabetes management**, ensuring better health outcomes and an improved quality of life.
